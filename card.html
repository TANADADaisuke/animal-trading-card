Japanese Killifish

A small fish that lives in a creek, sung by a Japanese nursery rhyme, "Medaka no Gakko".

  • Scientific Name: Oryzias latipes
  • Average Length: 3 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 3 years
  • Habitat: Temperate paddy creeks

Prefers shallow streams with shallow water. WIth eyes facing up, They are suitable for eating small insects on the surface of water. Spawns in the summer when the water temperature rises, and lays eggs in aquatic plants.
